# Attestly grant proposals

Two parallel submissions, scoped for **non-overlapping deliverables**:

## 1. `stf-proposal.md` — Sovereign Tech Fund (€100k)

- **What it funds**: open commons artefacts only — Rust core, Python + TypeScript SDKs, CLI, WASM verifier, specifications (Decision Schema, Checkpoint format, Verifier protocol), independent security review, standards-body engagement.
- **Licence**: Apache-2.0 (code) + CC-BY-4.0 (specs), in perpetuity.
- **Decision timeline**: ~10 weeks from submission (rolling).
- **Submission portal**: https://www.sovereign.tech/programs/fund.
- **Submission target**: Sat 31 May 2026.

## 2. `eic-step1.md` — EIC Accelerator Step 1 (short application)

- **What it funds (indirectly)**: GO from Step 1 unlocks ANI Voucher Deep Tech (€60k + €10k) mechanically. Step 2 (full application) would fund €1.8M grant + €1.2M optional equity for the **commercial-layer scale-up** (hosted dashboard, EU market entry, team build-out, sales) — built *on top of* the open commons artefacts, never inside them.
- **Licence boundary**: Step 1 / Step 2 funding stays on the commercial side; the open libraries remain Sovereign-Tech-funded Apache-2.0 + CC-BY-4.0.
- **Decision timeline**: ~4–6 weeks for Step 1 GO/NO-GO; Step 2 takes 6–9 months total.
- **Submission portal**: EU Funding & Tenders Portal (https://ec.europa.eu/info/funding-tenders/opportunities/portal/).
- **Submission target**: next monthly cut-off after 31 May 2026.

## Non-overlap statement (required by both funders)

Sovereign Tech Fund and EIC Accelerator fund **disjoint deliverables**:

- Sovereign Tech Fund → open-source library, CLI, verifier, spec, security audit, standards engagement.
- EIC Accelerator → commercial hosted dashboard, sales team, market entry, pilot subsidies, growth funding.

Both proposals reference each other for transparency. The open artefacts funded by Sovereign Tech Fund are perpetually free under Apache-2.0 + CC-BY-4.0; the commercial layer is built atop them under separate licensing and separate funding. There is no double-funding of any artefact.
